💩 Rules
🎯 Goal
Be first to get rid of all cards. Last player with cards is the Shithead 💩!
🃏 Setup
Each player gets 3 secret face-down cards + 6 hand cards. Place 3 hand cards face-up on your table spots, keep 3 in hand.

💡 Place your highest cards face-up!
▶️ Playing
Card order (low → high): A · 3 · 4 · 5 · 6 · 7 · 8 · 9 · J · Q · K · 2

• Play a card strictly higher than the top of pile
• Same rank = never allowed (including 7 on 7, 2 on 2)
• Can't play? Pick up the whole pile
• Draw back to 3 cards after each play
• Hand empty + draw empty → face-up cards move to your hand
• Face-up gone → flip face-down blind (can't play = take it + pile)
✨ Special Cards
2 Always playable on anything (except another 2). Stays as the new top — next player must beat a 2, which is almost impossible (only a 10 works).
3 Transparent. Playable on anything except another 3. Next player beats the card below the 3.
7 Next player must play strictly lower than 7 (A, 3, 4, 5, or 6). Then normal rules resume.
10 Burns the pile 🔥 Play again!
4× Four of a kind also burns 🔥 Play again!

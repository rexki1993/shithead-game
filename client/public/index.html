💩 How to Play
🎯 Goal
Be the first to get rid of ALL your cards. The last player still holding cards is the Shithead 💩 and loses!
🃏 Setup
Each player gets:
• 3 face-down cards — placed on the table, kept secret (even from you!)
• 6 hand cards — choose 3 to place face-up on top of your face-down cards. Keep 3 in hand.
Put your best (high) cards face-up!
▶️ Playing
• Play a card strictly higher than the top card of the pile
• You can play multiple cards of the same rank at once
• After playing, draw back up to 3 cards (while draw pile lasts)
• Can't play? Pick up the whole pile!
• When your hand is empty, play your face-up cards
• When face-up is gone, play your face-down cards blind (no peeking!)
✨ Special Cards
2 — Play on anything. Resets pile: next player can play any card.
3 — Transparent / Invisible. Can be played on anything except another 3. The next player must beat the card below the 3, as if the 3 wasn't there.
7 — Next player must play strictly lower than 7 (no 7 allowed). After that, normal rules resume.
10 — Burns the entire pile. Play again!
4× — Four cards of the same rank also burns the pile. Play again!
💡 Tips
• Place high cards (A, K, Q) face-up — you'll need them later
• Save your 10s and 2s for tough situations
• If someone plays a high card you can't beat, use a 2 or 10!
